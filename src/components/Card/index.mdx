# Card

A card is a flexible and extensible content container. It includes options for headers and footers, a wide variety of content, contextual background colors and powerful display options.

Cards are built with as little markup and styles as possible, but still manage to deliver a ton of control and customization. Built with flexbox, they offer easy alignment and mix well with other Bootstrap components. They have no margin by default, so use spacing utilities as needed.

Below is an example of a basic card with mixed content and a fixed width. Cards have no fixed width to start, so they’ll naturally fill the full width of its parent element. This is easily customized with our various sizing options.

## Variations

Cards support a wide variety of content, including images, text, list groups, links, and more. Below are examples of what’s supported.

### Body

The building block of a card is the `.card-body.` Use it whenever you need a padded section within a card.

<ComponentPreview>
	<Card style={{ width: 330 }}>

			<p>This is some text within a card body.</p>
		</Card.Body>
	</Card>
</ComponentPreview>

</div>

### Titles, Text and Links

Card titles are used by adding `.card-title` to a `<h*>` tag. In the same way, links are added and placed next to each other by adding `.card-link` to an `<a>` tag.

Subtitles are used by adding a `.card-subtitle` to a `<h*>` tag. If the `.card-title` and the `.card-subtitle` items are placed in a `.card-body` item, the card title and subtitle are aligned nicely.

<ComponentPreview>
<Card style={{ width: 330 }}>
    <Card.Body>
        <Card.Title as="p" className="h3 pt-0 mt-0">Card title</Card.Title>
        <Card.Subtitle as="h4" className="text-muted">Card subtitle</Card.Subtitle>
        <Card.Text>
            Some text to make up
            the bulk of the card's content.
        </Card.Text>
        <a href="#">View More</a>
    </Card.Body>
</Card>
</ComponentPreview>

<CodePreview>
<HtmlCodeBlock>

```html
<div class="card">
    <div class="card-body">
        <p class="card-title h3 pt-0 mt-0">Card title</p>
        <h4 class="card-subtitle mb-2 text-muted">Card subtitle</h4>
        <p class="card-text">Some text to make up the bulk of the card's content.</p>
        <a href="#">View More</a>
    </div>
</div>
```

</HtmlCodeBlock>
